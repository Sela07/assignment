Biography

Cristiano Ronaldo was born on February 5, 1985, in Funchal, Portugal. Many people think he's one of the best soccer players ever. He started his career at Sporting CP and then played for big clubs like Manchester United, Real Madrid, and Juventus. He's known for scoring a lot of goals and has won many awards, including five FIFA Ballon d'Or trophies. Besides soccer, Ronaldo also helps others and has his own businesses
